Games & Activities

From Upper Amity to the Night Woods!

🎲

Board & Card Games at Upper Amity

Upper Amity is the go-to spot for indoor strategy. Grab your cabin mates for a round of **Sequence**, **Exploding Kittens**, or classic card games like **Uno** and **Spoons**. It's the perfect place to cool off and challenge your brain.

🏊

Pool Time

Beat the Indiana heat with daily swim sessions! Whether you're doing cannonballs or participating in organized pool games, the pool is a camp staple for every session.

⚽

Field Games

Head out to the field for high-energy recreation. We play everything from **Soccer** and **Basketball** to unique cabin competitions and group recreation that uses every bit of our 158 acres.

🔦

Night Games

The real fun starts after the sun goes down! Night games typically run from **9:30 PM to 10:30 PM** and include camp-wide adventures like **Mission Impossible**, **Capture the Flag**, and **Counselor Hunt**.
