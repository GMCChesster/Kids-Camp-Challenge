📝 The "Camp Rules" Summary:

  • The Flip-Flop Rule: Flip-flops are ONLY for the pool and bathhouse. Closed-toe shoes are required everywhere else!
  • Modesty Policy: All tops must have at least 1-inch straps and cover your stomach at all times.
  • Swimwear: If you wear a two-piece, you must wear a dark t-shirt over it.
  • Snack Storage: If you bring snacks, they MUST be in tightly-sealing containers to keep bugs away!
